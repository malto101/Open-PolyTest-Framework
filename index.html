    PolyOnTest¶

    Embedded-first unit and on-target tests for C, C++, and Rust — from a two-file MCU drop-in to host CI — without a vendor RTOS or build-system lock-in.

  dut[DUT harness] -->|PTWP stream| cli[Host CLI]
  cli --> reports[Console / JUnit / JSON]
